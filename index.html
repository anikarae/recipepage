Strawberry Cream Cheese Cookies

with white chocolate chips

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of strawberries
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 3/4 cup of flour
  • 1 tsp of baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 cup of unsalted butter (room temperature)
  • 4 ounces of cream cheese
  • 3/4 cup of granulated sugar
  • 2 tsp of vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 6 ounces of white chocolate chips

Directions:

  1. Dice 2 cups of strawberries
  2. Put 1 tbsp of lemon juice (on the berries)
  3. In a bowl, combine 1 3/4 cup of flour, 1 tsp of baking soda, 1/4 tsp of salt and sift together.
  4. In a different mixing bowl:
    • 1/4 cup of unsalted butter (room temp)
    • 4 ounces of cream cheese
    Mix together
  5. While mixing in the bowl, add:
    • 3/4 cup of granulated sugar
    • 2 tsp of vanilla extract
    • 2 large eggs
  6. Then add dry ingredients from before and mix
  7. Add 6 ounces of white chocolate chips
  8. Drain strawberries and add 2 tbsp of flour to coat
  9. Fold in strawberries into the cookie dough
  10. Refrigerate for 10 minutes
  11. Scoop onto trays and bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes or until golden brown

Note: This is a recipe i made myself a long time ago! I hope whoever tries to make them, loves them as much as i do. My family begs me to make these for them for every holiday so they are extra special! *(Might look like they aren’t done but sometimes they just need to be taken out of the oven to sit for awhile.)*
